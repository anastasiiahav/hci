Excursion Reflect

I was really excited to go to the excursion, to see existing creatures and to get some inspiration for the lab weeks. I've learned a lot about what artists made what they used to create theirs artworks. Also the idea behind each creation is mesmering.
The projects what spoke to me the most were The Woodpecker and Second Livestock. To my opinion these were one of the greatest artworks. Mostly because of combining the nature and the artificial creatures. Also the Warkatower was pretty great.
The things what didn't spoke to me as much were The Weather War and Human Sensor. I was not excited about these projects.
Robotanica was valuable for sure. I have seen a lot of innovative projects and met interesting people. I would love to go to the next exhibition to meet new creatures and artists. I would choose for an other place for the exhibition next time. The place looked great, but the weather was the biggest problem. I lot of people left before the ending of Robotanica (me as well) because it was too cold and too rainy. Because of the weather I couldn't 100% enjoy this amazing exhibition.

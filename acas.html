Assignments Artificial Creatures

For this assignment I had to come up with a concept where a homeostatis model should apply to a device. From the given list I have chosen for a bicycle. Here below you can see my concept.

Smartie

I came up with an idea for kids. Smartie is a new generation bicycle which is made to help kids to learn how to cycle. Sometimes the process of learning could be hard for a child, so why don’t make a something that will help them not just to learn faster but also enjoy the process without wounds and cuts?
This bicycle has a build in sensors to avoid obstacles and people, to help keep balance, to recognize the light, so that bike lights turn on automatically when it’s dark and turn off when it’s daytime. Smartie could also recognize the owner, what makes him act like an artificial creature, so its opens the lock. This way the learning process will be fun and fast.
Smart bicycle could also have eyes and talk to the people. Like when he sees his owner, he could sing the happy song and blink with his eyes. This makes him act even more as an artificial creature.
For the performing he needs to be charged. It could get his power from the sun, what is environment friendly.

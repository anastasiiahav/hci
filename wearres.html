For this assignment I had to look for nteresting and impressive projects. The Wearable project of my choice you can look here below.

Tap

Tap is a wearable which work as an input device, created by Roger Chang. It’s the new generation keyboard. Tap uses your fingers to control and communicate with the digital world. Each tap or combination of fingers is different character. For example to type the letter ‘A’, you need to tap you’re your numb. Tap can be used not just as keyboard, but as mouse as well. It doesn’t use QUERTY mode, it has its unique mode. You can connect Tap to your phone, laptop, tablet, deskrop and even smart TV.

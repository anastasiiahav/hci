Lab Weeks Product

The Spider
This is an artificial creature what is created to trigger different emotions, like fear and anxiety. Although it doesn't look as scary as we planned and it doesn't have legs, but it still does look like a spider.
This spider reacts at the obstacles and people, buy going backwards to avoid it. Therefore we have used a proxomity sensor.
The Spider is created to not be controlled by the user. The user has no need in it. The Spider is more like interactive toy.

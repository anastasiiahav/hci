Assignments AR&VR

During the lecture I have seen several applications of virtual and augmented reality. For this assignment I should choosee 3 application areas from the given list and think of a VR or AR concept for each application area. Here below you can find my concepts.

Education

There are lots of ways how AR and VR could be used in education process. One of ideas could be to use it in the books to give better explanation with interactive examples. In place of boring text about for example monkeys that need to count her bananas there would be an actual monkey playing with bananas and asking for the help of the child. Kids just need to point their smartphones to the page where indicated and wait for it. This way kids will use their phones for something nice and educational.

Healthcare

Healthcare could also use AR and VR. One of ideas could be to use it in place of instruction for the medication. User could point with his phone at the box to reveal the virtual instruction. Virtual doctor could show how and when this medication could be used (and when should not) with ‘real’ examples. This way medication boxes could be smaller, because they don’t need extra space for the instruction. Also, this way we can help nature by not destroying as much trees, as we normally do to make paper.

Museums

Some people found museums pretty boring. What I find boring about museums is the way the explanations are brought to the viewer, like boring texts next to the subject or the audio story through the earphones. AR and VR could be a better way to deliver information about the project to the people. People on the paintings or their creators could come out of their canvas to tell the story. This way museum could possibly attract more new visitors.

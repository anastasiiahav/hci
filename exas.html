Assigment Exertion Intefaces

During this topic I had to came up with my own concept for Exertion Interfaces. Here below you can read my idea for the new students and employees of THUAS to get to know the building faster.

Get to Know THUAS

Get to Know THUAS is a concept for new students and employees who are going to start their journey at The Hague University of Applied Sciences. On the big screen next to the entrance will be projected a picture and description of the place. Each person had to guess what for place this could be and start search for it. The first person to find the place has to push ‘virtual button’ and get points. How faster you get to the place, how more points you are going to get. And this continued till of the most important places is shown. With the help of this game new people can learn THUAS faster and prevent some losses.
This concept just needs couple of AR projectors, one for main projector, where the score board and assignments are going to be places, and couple of other places, which people need to find and push a button.

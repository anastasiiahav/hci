Research Artificial Creatures

For this assignment I had to look for nteresting and impressive projects. The VR project of my choice you can look here below.

Aibo

Sony came up with the new version of Aibo. Aibo is robotic dog with personality. He is able to not just recognize people but recognize their emotions as well. He remembers what made his owner happy last time and recreates it next time to make his owner happy again. He plays and moves around with boundless energy, almost as a puppy would.
Aibo has building sensors to avoid objects, respond to touch and even takes his own pictures which you can look later. It has 4,000 parts, 22 actuators and OLED-screen eyes to more realistically duplicate dog activity.

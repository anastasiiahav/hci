Research AR&VR

For this assignment I had to look for nteresting and impressive projects. The VR project of my choice you can look here below.

The Displaced

There millions and millions of amazing projects created with help of VR and AR, but I choose the one which is one of the most powerful VR videos on my opinion. This amazing project called The Displaced. It’s a VR film, created in 2015 by The New York Times and Within, that doesn’t require your active participation, but indeed requires your observation and attention. This film is about the life of three children from Syria, South Sudan and Ukraine. The children who were forced to leave their homes due to war. This project is created so that you could put yourself in theirs shoes, to feel how is it to be a child in the war.

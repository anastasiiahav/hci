Reflect Wearables

Before the lecture of this topic, I haven’t been too much excited about the wearables, but the lecture did inspire me to do some research and maybe choose this subject to work during the lab weeks.
Wearables are the future. I believe that in the future we are not going to see our life without it. Wearables could be solutions to so many problems that we are having daily.
I didn’t expect too much from this subject until the lecture. I was super excited to start the workshop and see if this is going to be the topic I will choose to work in Zoetermeer, because it’s the topic what inspirate me the most till now.
